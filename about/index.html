The Story

In 2017, we were founded by a Big Kid with big dreams. Fresh out of college, NTS was built on the idea that creating impactful software comes from being intensely inquisitive while remaining adventurous. That dream remains 5 years later, as NTS has grown a culture embracing creativity, fun, and exploration. With age (and a couple of gray hairs) comes wisdom, but its clear the spirit of adventure is still alive. Most importantly, NTS still defines success as building software that has a profound impact for their clients.

Adventure

Childlike Spirit
