Design & build mobile apps for both Android and iOS.

We understand the challenges of building a mobile app. You’ll need your app to work on multiple devices, follow new policies released by iOS and android, and be approved by the app store deployment process.

We have extensive experience developing native apps for both android and iOS as well as using cross-platform technologies like Xamarin to speed up the development process and decrease maintenance costs.

Design and build cloud based web applications.

A powerful web app can be incredibly impactful for an organization. We use technologies like .Net core, Angular, and Vue.JS to create an easily accessible solution. Your employees will be able to seamlessly work from anywhere.

Developing a responsive web app for the cloud is effective; It can substantially drive efficiency without having to leave your desk.

Migrate to the Cloud or re-write legacy apps cloud-first.

Whether you're looking to migrate legacy apps to the cloud or understand how to better develop with the cloud in mind, our AWS certified engineers can help.

Dedicated Teams

Finding & managing technology talent can be difficult and expensive. We solve these challenges by providing onshore/offshore dedicated development teams supported by a range of highly specialized ad hoc skills that only a diverse group could provide.

With this approach, we are able to quickly scale to meet increased demand while remaining cost-effective. A dedicated team means you won’t have to spend time recruiting and retaining hard-to-find talent, and instead can focus on growing your business.

Healthcare Solutions

We have years of experience in developing health applications and intuitive software for medical practices, hospitals, and other healthcare businesses. We’ve built mobile apps for clinicians in the field, a telemedicine platform, and web-based solutions to house highly-sensitive patient data.

We have repeatedly built software and apps that meet HIPAA’s regulatory demands, and understand the unique challenges thereof.
